   </p>
<ul>
<li>

    </li>
<li>

<li>
     Experiment with new hardware: in-dash systems, BLE beacons, parking access control equipment, etc, and keep us at the cutting edge
    </li>
<li>
     Assess the complexity, feasibility, and impact of integrations with potential partners and vendors.
    </li>
<li>
     Prototype new ideas and validate them with users
    </li>
<li>

<li>
