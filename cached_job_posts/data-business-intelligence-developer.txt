<u>
     Data/Business Intelligence Developer Core Job Responsibilities
    </u>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
     Designs, creates, and supports transactional databases for critical business systems; responsible for the overall data management, data quality and delivery of business reporting needs.
    </li>
<li>
     Writes, debugs, upgrades, and scales efficient queries, views, indexes, stored procedures and functions within SQL Server.
    </li>
<li>
     Works closely with Customer Insights Manager, to help design, develop and maintain advanced reporting, analytics, dashboards and other BI solutions using either PowerBI or Tableau and other reporting /visualization tools.
    </li>
<li>
     Proactively assists in locating and resolving problems in data and also resolves production database issues.
    </li>
<li>
     Speaks with business users about requirements and data structures in a way that is high level and not confusing, while also possessing the skill to speak with highly technical resources.
    </li>
</ul>
