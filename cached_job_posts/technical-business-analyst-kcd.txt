<u>
     Overview:
    </u>

<div>
<p>
     kCura is a global company with team members who are driven by our customers to build exceptional software for them to use every day. Our product, called Relativity, handles large volumes of data and helps corporations, law firms, and government agencies solve their own unique data problems.
    </p>
<p>
     The way we work together is centered on our core values of collaborating, communicating, pushing to exceed expectations (even our own), being humble, and having fun while we do it. We enjoy ourselves,give back, and work (and play) hard together. If this sounds like the place for you, check out the details of this position below.
    </p>
<p>
     As a member of kCura’s Custom Development group, you will work with customers and internal teams to deliver solutions that help fill the gaps in Relativity functionality and the desired workflow. You will be required to have a full technical understanding of the Relativity Platform. You will gather detailed business requirements, understand the request and provide wireframe or prototyped solutions for confirmation of requirements. You will work closely with the developers to ensure they have a complete understanding of the requirements for the solutions and deliver within the time estimated. Once the solution has been delivered, you will be responsible for standard kCura Level 1 type support. You may be required to handle technical support calls after hours about seven times a year.
    </p>
