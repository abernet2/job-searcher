    <b>
     Job Description
    </b>
</p>
<p>
    The Senior Microsoft Web Developer - Managed Services will be responsible for building and maintaining internal and external facing web applications for Rightpoint's clients using the Microsoft “stack” including ASP.NET as well as client-side browser technologies (e.g., JavaScript). This person will work independently with the managed services team of Engineers, Developers, and Technical No-Code consultants to develop and enhance previously developed applications, and problem solve issues within client environments.
   </p>
<p>
    This is a role within our Managed Services team, whose goal is to keep client environments running smoothly.  This means that the people in this group need to thrive on being in a fast-paced environment where priorities can shift quickly, be very good at problem-solving, and have outstanding client-management skills. You will be supporting custom applications/solutions that have been previously delivered to Rightpoint’s clients, helping them further extend and/or maintain functionality to accommodate their ever-changing business requirements and processes. In this role, you will be switching context frequently as you will be working on multiple projects at different stages of the project lifecycle simultaneously. It is important that the applicant have a strong background in developing and supporting scalable, maintainable, high-quality, and high performing web applications using the Microsoft technology stack.
   </p>
