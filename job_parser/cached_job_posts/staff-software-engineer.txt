     Essential Responsibilities
    </span>
<span>
     In this role, you will:
     <br/>
</span>
</div>
<span>
<p>
     Engage with global service organizations and L4 in testing customer issues in Solutions lab Build and maintain EIS Software Solutions in 3 labs (Barrington, Bangalore and Milwaukee). Includes – install, configure, upgrade, integrate, viability and verification test 8 or more reference architectures (solutions) comprised of multiple EIS products and components.
     <br/>
</p>
<li>
     Perform impact assessment on solutions from changes in upstream products, especially cross-product dependencies that can potentially break a solution.
     <br/>
</li>
<li>
     Document solution level install and upgrade best practices and work arounds for issues identified during FAT testing.  Drive solution related requirements, gaps and enhancements in upstream products working with architecture and product teams.
     <br/>
</li>
<li>
     Perform deal reviews to identify variation from standard reference architectures and assess impact for viability of the proposed solution configuration being sold.
     <br/>
</li>
<li>
     Drive automation in testing of solutions using automated test harness.
     <br/>
</li>
<li>
     Drive service productivity at a solution level by automating, reducing and clarifying steps for installation, upgrade, configuration and integration working with product and serviceability teams. Execute in a fast pace delivery mode and focus in delivering tasks to meet the product release goal Lead and provide guidance to a team of 5 to 7 remote engineers with planning and execution of solution FAT testing using agile methodologies such as SCRUM
