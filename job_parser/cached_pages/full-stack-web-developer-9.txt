<div class="nj-span-left-content adjusted-margin">
 <div class="job-top-header-wrapper">
  <span class="nj-job-body">
   <p>
    Want to create web solutions that deliver our message and 
our software to customers and prospects every day?  Want to help support 
and improve the systems that drives our business?
   </p>
   <p>
    We are looking for a highly-motivated, skilled developer 
to fill the role of Web Developer.  As a Web Developer, you will work 
closely with Product Management, a Graphic Artist, and Marketing to create and 
deliver critical website updates. You will be relied upon to produce 
easy-to-use, dependable web solutions to support our products.  You will 
create integrations between critical business solutions that power sales, 
finance, marketing and development.
   </p>
   <p>
    In this position you must be proficient at maintaining 
and building web solutions, work well with teams, and be an effective 
communicator. You will be interacting with multiple departments on a daily 
basis to solve problems, not taking predefined requirements.  In addition, 
you should be flexible both in development skills and variety of tasks assigned.
   </p>
   <p>
    We at Microsystems are strong believers in a 
healthy work-life balance with added benefits you don’t find everywhere. Work 
hours are flexible, and there are many opportunities to relax and get to know 
each other, such as sports leagues, Thirsty Thursdays, and chair massages.   From Cinco de Mayo to Halloween to Festivus,
there is always a reason to share a meal and celebrate! Microsystems cares 
about all employees; we offer an annual onsite Wellness Event, our work 
environment and attire are very casual, and free drinks and fruit are provided 
daily. Meetings are minimal and you are always appreciated by management. We 
are dedicated to giving back to the community. The team participates in an 
Annual 5K Walk/Run event for charity as well as Community Service Projects and 
Team Building Events.
    <strong>
    </strong>
   </p>
   <p>
    <b>
     KEY RESPONSIBILITES
    </b>
   </p>
   <ul>
    <li>
     Analyzing
     and executing requirements
    </li>
    <li>
     Maintaining
     and building new Salesforce integrations
    </li>
    <li>
     Constructing
     an enterprise web solution from the ground up
    </li>
    <li>
     Assessing
     new web technologies for use with the site
    </li>
    <li>
     Leading
     backend architecture design
    </li>
    <li>
     Implementing
     front end designs
    </li>
    <li>
     Integrating
     databases and scripts with a CMS
    </li>
    <li>
     Utilizing
     Agile methodologies to scope, estimate, and execute work
    </li>
   </ul>
   <p>
    <b>
     WHAT YOU WON'T BE DOING
    </b>
   </p>
   <ul>
    <li>
     Heavily
     creating content
    </li>
    <li>
     Graphic
     design
    </li>
   </ul>
   <p>
    <b>
     REQUIREMENTS
    </b>
   </p>
   <ul>
    <li>
     Minimum
     of 5 years of experience in full stack web development or comparable
     talent
    </li>
    <li>
     Proficiency
     with the following: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AJAX, JavaScript Frameworks,
     IIS, Microsoft SQL Server, ASP.net, WordPress or comparable CMS
    </li>
    <li>
     Salesforce
     API and PHP experience
    </li>
   </ul>
   <p>
    <b>
    </b>
    <b>
     BONUS POINTS
    </b>
   </p>
   <ul>
    <li>
     Sitecore
     development experience
    </li>
    <li>
     Hubspot
     familiarity
    </li>
   </ul>
   <p>
   </p>
   <p>
    <b>
     WHO WE ARE
    </b>
   </p>
   <p>
    Microsystems designs and develops cutting edge software
solutions for organizations whose craft is creating highly complex business
documents. With a client base spanning the top 100 law firms as well as various
corporate legal and life sciences corporations, Microsystems is committed to
delivering quality products and services that increase profitability and
efficiency.
   </p>
